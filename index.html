Who I Am '&&' Why I Am Here

My name is Aubrey Young.

My goal is to use what I learn here to both enhance the JavaScript I'm currently learning in Object-Oriented Scripting, while also further increasing my website building capabilities. I've learned a decent amount of HTML and CSS from previous web design courses, and I recently have been building a website for a friend using Bootstrap. I have also dipped a toe in JavaScript to make certain actions happen on projects from previous courses, but I haven't dedicated to learning JS until I started the Object-Oriented Scripting class that I am in now.

An interesting fact about me is that this is my second time in college. My first time, I majored in Economics (concentration, Finance) and served in the Navy for 8 years. After serving, I decided to look for work that aligned with my BA in Economics; I found that job, but quickly realized in the 2 years that I worked there that though I was good with numbers, finance was not something I could see myself doing for the next few decades. However, I have always been fascinated and drawn to signage - whether it was advertisements, books, video games, toys, or clothing logos. I figured if I was going to make another huge life change after leaving the Navy, it might as well be toward something I would want to do for the rest of my life, even with no amateur or professional training prior to attending Ai. I'm in my third quarter now and I love what I've been learning, what I've been doing, and have even managed to pull down a couple of small web and logo jobs in my spare time.
